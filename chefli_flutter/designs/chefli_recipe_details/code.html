Pan-Seared Scallops

schedule 25 min
bar_chart Medium
local_fire_department 450 kcal

Ingredients

2 servings

Instructions

1

Prep Scallops

Remove the side muscle from the scallops. Pat them very dry with paper towels to ensure a good sear.

2

Heat the Pan

Add oil to a large skillet over high heat until it just begins to smoke. Salt the scallops just before cooking.

3

The Sear

Place scallops in the pan. Do not crowd. Sear for 2 minutes without moving until a golden crust forms.
